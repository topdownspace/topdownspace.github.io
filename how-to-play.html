How to Play

Use WASD keys to move your spaceship through the battle area. Player 2 uses arrow keys and player 3 uses IJKL keys. Use Q, /, U to dash and E, shift, O to change formations. To grow your fleet (gain more ships) run into the blue powerups scatterd across the map. Avoid asteroids, obstacles and enemy fire from other players to stay alive!

Game Objective

Your mission in TopDownSpace is to be the last one standing. It's a good idea to prioritize power-ups for end-game though

Basic Strategies

Here are some tips to improve your gameplay:

  • Go for power ups: Esspeccially during endgame it helps to have the numbers
  • Stay Mobile: Keep moving to dodge enemy fire and asteroids.
  • Use formations: Take advantage of your formation key! Formations can be really defensive or quite offensive.
