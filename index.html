HOW TO PLAY

- Move: Use WASD (Player 1), Arrow Keys (Player 2), or IJKL (Player 3).
- Formation: Press E, Shift, or O to cycle formations.
- Dash: Press Q, /, or U (2s cooldown).
- Shooting: Automatic based on ship count.
- Power-Ups: Collect +2 to +20 ships to grow your fleet.
- Avoid: Steer clear of asteroids and obstacles.
- Win: Be the last survivor!

How to Play

- Move: Use WASD (Player 1), Arrow Keys (Player 2), or IJKL (Player 3).
- Formation: Press E, Shift, or O to cycle through formations (Diamond, Compact, Swarm, etc.).
- Dash: Press Q, /, or U for a quick speed boost (2s cooldown).
- Shooting: Your fleet shoots automatically; more ships mean more firepower.
- Power-Ups: Collect glowing orbs labeled +2 to +20 to add ships to your fleet.
- Avoid: Dodge asteroids and obstacles to survive.
- Win: Outlast all opponents to claim victory!
- Pause: Press 'P' to pause/unpause the game.

About

Swarm Wars is a thrilling top-down space combat game where you command a fleet of ships against AI or other players. Battle through asteroid fields, collect power-ups, and outlast your opponents to claim victory! Made by Horseywin.
